The Diversity of American Meat Washing Habits before Cooking.

Do people wash meat before cooking? What do they think they are trying to accomplish by washing their meat? What are some of the main factors behind why americans wash or don't wash their meat?

YouTuber (and former public radio reporter) Adam Ragusea asked his viewers to answer a detailed survey about whether (and why, and how) they wash meat before cooking it. You can check out his video here. Our visualization uses the data collected by Ragusea and aims to serve as an exploratory experience to visualize different meat washing habits. We will be showcasing only the responses from American residents.
